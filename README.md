## 🏦 Managing Bank Reconciliations

Reconciliation ensures your bank records match your internal bookkeeping records. Perform this task every month to maintain clean books.

1. Open the Banking menu at the top of the program.
2. Select Reconcile from the drop-down list.
3. Select the bank account you wish to reconcile.
4. Enter the ending balance from your physical bank statement.
5. Enter any service charges or interest earned.
6. Compare the transactions in the software to the transactions on your statement.
7. Check off each matching transaction.
8. Click Reconcile Now when the difference equals zero.
9. Print or save the reconciliation report for your records.

## 💾 Setting Up Automated Backups

Data loss causes significant disruptions to business operations. Use the built-in backup tool to schedule regular copies of your financial data.

1. Go to the File menu.
2. Select Back Up Company and then choose Create Local Backup.
3. Select Local Backup in the menu and click Options.
4. Choose the specific folder where you want to store your backup files.
5. Enable the option to schedule future backups.
6. Pick a recurring frequency, such as daily or weekly.
7. Set a specific time when you typically finish your daily tasks.
8. Click Finish to save your settings.
9. Test your backup process once to confirm it works correctly.
